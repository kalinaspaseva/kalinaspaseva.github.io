One of my hobbies that plays a really big part in my life is Folk dancing! A folk dance is a dance that reflects the life of the people of a certain country or region and almost every young kid in my country goes to folk dancing classes at least a year in their lives.However only for a few of us folk dancing becomes a part of our lifestyle and for me, it was a part of my life for 8 years!

It is sure an experience of a lifetime, considering the number of countries I have visited during these 8 years, as well as the friends i have made from all around the world and the many things I have learnt about the cultures of the European and Asian countries i have come in contact with. It's such a lovely experience and I don't think anything can replace those moments I have spent with these people.
